How to drive it

Click any two stations to run a trip between them (or use the dropdowns + Run).
Try a same-line branch end to the other (backtrack pause at the junction), or two ends on different lines (color morph at each transfer).
Unnamed traces label stations by coordinate.

Legend

◉ station   ◆ junction (branch split)   ⇄ transfer (line change).
Scroll/drag to zoom & pan. Color is derived from the line name.
